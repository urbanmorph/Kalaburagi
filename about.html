Frequently Asked Questions

‚ñº
Why should I trust this dashboard? Past promises were broken. +

Fair question. Here's what's different this time:

  • Public data: You can see real-time progress, not just press releases
  • Independent verification: 20% of projects randomly checked by external auditors
  • Money trail: Every rupee tracked (sanctioned ‚Üí released ‚Üí utilized)
  • Honest reporting: Red alerts show what's failing, not just successes
  • Your feedback: Dashboard has "Report Issue" button - we respond in 7 days
Will this actually create 100,000 jobs or just paper jobs? +

We track two types of jobs:

  • Formal jobs (high confidence üü¢): ESIC registrations - verified by government system
  • Informal jobs (medium confidence üü°): Surveys + multiplier estimates

Both are clearly labeled on dashboard. We don't claim certainty where we don't have it. Current progress: 14,200 jobs created (verified via ESIC + field surveys).

I'm a farmer. How will I actually benefit? +

Depends on your situation:

  • Rainfed land: Get borewell (‚Çπ3 lakh value, you pay ‚Çπ30,000-1.5L depending on category)
  • Growing tur: Join FPO, sell directly to upgraded mill (‚Çπ200/quintal extra)
  • Landless: Your children get free training, factory jobs (‚Çπ15,000/month starting)

Timeline: Borewells in 2 years, FPOs in 3 years, jobs in 5 years.

What if the government changes in next election? +

We've designed for continuity:

  • Multi-party buy-in: MLAs from all parties attended planning workshops
  • Locked-in funding: ‚Çπ11,000 cr already committed via Central schemes/NABARD (can't be easily reversed)
  • Public pressure: This dashboard makes it hard to quietly cancel - everyone is watching
  • Legal MoUs: Signed agreements with banks, NABARD (penalties if govt backs out)
Why rank 15? Why not aim for rank 1? +

Honesty over hype:

  • Rank 1 (Bangalore Urban) has ‚Çπ5.83L per capita - would need ‚Çπ115,000 crore investment (unrealistic)
  • Rank 15 (‚Çπ2.10L) is ambitious but achievable with ‚Çπ15,500 cr
  • Better to under-promise and over-deliver than the opposite

Once we hit rank 15, we'll set the next target: Top 10 by 2035.

How can I verify the data is real? +

Three ways to verify:

  1. Cross-check sources: We link to original documents (Economic Survey, NABARD reports)
  2. Visit projects: Geo-tagged photos include GPS coordinates - go see for yourself
  3. RTI requests: All data is RTI-compliant, request raw data from Collectorate
What if my village is not getting any scheme? +

Contact your BDO (Block Development Officer) - find contact in "Who's Responsible" section above. Each block has minimum allocation guaranteed.

If genuinely left out, escalate to PMU helpline. We track block-wise equity on the dashboard to ensure fair distribution.
