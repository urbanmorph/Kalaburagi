#### **3. Evidence-Based Decision Making**

*Philosophy:* Opinions are important, but data decides.

*Practice:*
- Every major decision (>Ã¢â€šÂ¹10 cr impact) must be backed by:
  - Dashboard indicators (quantitative trends)
  - Field reports (qualitative ground truth)
  - Beneficiary surveys (voice of people)
  - Expert opinion (technical feasibility)

#### **4. Continuous Feedback Loops**

*Philosophy:* Monitoring is not a one-way street. Beneficiaries must have a voice.

*Mechanism 1: Dashboard Feedback Button*

*Mechanism 3: Beneficiary Committees*

- Recommendations published on dashboard
